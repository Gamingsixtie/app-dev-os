### Human Verification Required

#### 1. SchoolNameDialog visual and interaction check

**Test:** Open the app, click "School toevoegen" on the school overview page.
**Expected:** A modal dialog appears with title "Nieuwe school", a labelled text field "Schoolnaam", a disabled "Start wizard" button when the field is empty, and an "Annuleren" button. After typing a name, "Start wizard" becomes active. Clicking it navigates to `/scholen/$slug/wizard/1` using the typed name.
**Why human:** React modal rendering, Zod live validation, and disabled button state require DOM interaction.

#### 2. SchoolCard link destination for incomplete school

**Test:** With an incomplete school in the overview (badge "Niet voltooid" visible), click the school card.
**Expected:** Browser navigates to `/scholen/$slug/wizard/1`, not to `/scholen/$slug`.
**Why human:** TanStack Router conditional navigation cannot be verified without a running browser.

#### 3. DashboardTab incomplete school banner

**Test:** Navigate directly to `/scholen/$slug` for a school where `isComplete = false` in Supabase.
**Expected:** The full dashboard content is replaced by an amber-colored banner with heading "Schoolprofiel is nog niet voltooid" and a prominent "Profiel voltooien" button. Clicking the button navigates to `/scholen/$slug/wizard/1`.
**Why human:** Requires a live Supabase connection returning `is_complete = false` for the school record.

#### 4. WizardPage context-aware header

**Test:** Open the wizard for a newly-created school (0 completed steps). Then open the wizard for a school that has previously completed at least one wizard step.
**Expected:** Fresh school shows h1 "Schoolprofiel instellen" with subtitle "Vul de basisgegevens van de school in...". Returning school shows "Prijsvergelijking voor uw school" with the full subtitle.
**Why human:** Requires `useSchool` hook to return different `completedSteps` arrays — needs running browser + Supabase.
