# Plan 18-01: DMU Role Model Upgrade, Klantreis Timeline & Dashboard Integration

## Objective

Upgrade the DMU position model from 4 roles (coordinator, mt, finance, overig) to 6 roles (beslisser, inkoper, adviseur, gebruiker, beinvloeder, overig), add a customer journey timeline view in ContactsTab, and integrate a klantreis summary card in the school dashboard.

## Context

- @18-RESEARCH.md (architecture patterns, migration strategy, pitfalls)
- @18-UI-SPEC.md (visual contract for all components)
