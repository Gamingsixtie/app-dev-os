<objective>
AI intake flow: streaming extraction from free-form notes with diff-view confirmation and append-only save.

Purpose: Accountmanagers can type conversation notes during a phone call, trigger AI analysis, review extracted data against existing school profile, edit extracted values if needed (per INTAKE-03), and selectively save new items. This is the core AI-powered workflow for capturing school intelligence during calls.
Output: IntakeModeToggle, StreamingExtraction, DiffView components (with editable fields), updated ConversationForm and ConversationsTab with AI-intake mode integration.
</objective>
