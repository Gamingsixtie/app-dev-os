# Microcopy Samples

> Real, approved in-app text from this app. Skills match this style when writing new copy.

## Buttons

- Primary: _"Save"_, _"Create"_, _"Send"_
- Secondary: _"Cancel"_, _"Discard"_, _"Skip"_
- Destructive: _"Delete"_, _"Remove"_

## Form labels

- _"Email"_ (not "Email address")
- _"Workspace name"_ (not "Name your workspace")
- _"Description (optional)"_

## Form helper text

- _"We'll send a magic link here."_
- _"Visible to all workspace members."_
- _"Up to 200 characters."_

## Empty states

- Inbox empty: _"No new messages. You're all caught up."_
- No projects: _"No projects yet. Create your first to get started."_ + button
- No results: _"Nothing matches '{query}'. Try a different search."_

## Errors

- Validation: _"Email needs an @ sign."_
- Network: _"Couldn't reach the server. Check your connection and try again."_
- Permissions: _"You don't have access to this. Ask your admin."_
- Not found: _"This page doesn't exist or you don't have access."_

## Confirmations

- Delete: _"Delete this project? This can't be undone."_
- Discard: _"Discard your changes?"_
- Leave: _"You have unsaved changes. Leave anyway?"_

## Success toasts

- _"Saved."_
- _"Invite sent."_
- _"Project deleted."_

## Onboarding

- Step 1 title: _"Welcome. Let's set up your workspace."_
- Step CTA: _"Create workspace"_
- Skip option: _"Skip"_

## Loading states

- Generic: _"Loading…"_
- Specific: _"Saving…"_, _"Building dashboard…"_
- Long: _"This usually takes ~30 seconds."_
